###### HOTARC2

HOTARC2 x, y, r, startangle, endangle, unID Status arc definition with its centerpoint at (x, y) from the angle startangle to endangle, with a radius of r. Status arc is an arc which is recognized by the intelligent cursor but it is not visible in itself. Can have a unique ID for associative dimensioning purpose.
