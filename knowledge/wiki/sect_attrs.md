###### SECT_ATTRS

SECT_ATTRS fill, fill_background_pen,

fill_pen, contour_pen [, line_type] Defines the attributes used for the cut part of the 3D elements in the Section/Elevation window. Compatibility: up to Archicad 19 the PROJECT2{3} command is affected too. Inline fill and line_type attributes (defined in master script or 3d script) are not accepted.

fill: fill name or index number. fill_background_pen: fill background pencolor number. fill_pen: fill pencolor number. contour_pen: fill contour pencolor number. line_type: line type of polygon edges.
