###### UI_CURRENT_PAGE

UI_CURRENT_PAGE index Definition of the current tabpage to display. Warning: Jumping to a non-existent page forces the insertion of a new page without buttons and controls, and therefore there is no possibility to go to any other page from there. index: valid index of the UI_PAGE to display.
