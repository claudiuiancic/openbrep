###### PRISM

PRISM n, h, x1, y1, ..., xn, yn Right prism with its base polygon in the x-y plane (see the parameters of the POLY command and the POLY_ command). The height along the z-axis is abs(h). Negative h values can also be used. In that case the second base polygon is below the x-y plane. Restriction of parameters:

n >= 3
