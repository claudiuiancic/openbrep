###### WALL_THICKNESS thickness of the wall

in case of inclined walls: the wall thickness at the opening axis (local z axis)

WALL_START_THICKNESS Start thickness of the wall WALL_END_THICKNESS End thickness of the wall WALL_INCL inclination of the wall surfaces

the angle between the two inclined wall surfaces - 0 for common straight walls

WALL_FLIPPED boolean value set according to "Reference Line Location: Flip Wall on Reference Line" option of

the wall
