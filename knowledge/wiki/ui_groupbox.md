###### UI_GROUPBOX

UI_GROUPBOX text, x, y, width, height A groupbox is a rectangular separator with caption text. It can be used to visually group logically related parameters.

text: the title of the groupbox. x, y: the position of upper left corner. width, height: width and height in pixels.
