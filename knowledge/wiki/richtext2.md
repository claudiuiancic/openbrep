###### RICHTEXT2

RICHTEXT2 x, y, textblock_name Place a previously defined TEXTBLOCK. For more details, see the TEXTBLOCK command.

x, y: X-Y coordinates of the richtext location. textblock_name: the name of a previously defined TEXTBLOCK

BINARY 2D
