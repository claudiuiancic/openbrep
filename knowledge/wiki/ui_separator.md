###### UI_SEPARATOR

UI_SEPARATOR x1, y1, x2, y2 Generates a separator rectangle. The rectangle becomes a single (vertical or horizontal) separator line if x1 = x2 or y1 = y2

x1, y1: upper left node coordinates (starting point coordinates of the line). x2, y2: lower right node coordinates (endpoint coordinates of the line).
