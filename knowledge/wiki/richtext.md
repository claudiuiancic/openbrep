###### RICHTEXT x, y,

height, 0, textblock_name A 3D representation of a previously defined TEXTBLOCK. For more details, see the TEXTBLOCK command.

- x, y: X-Y coordinates of the richtext location. height: thickness of the characters in meters. textblock_name: the name of a previously defined TEXTBLOCK. In the current version of GDL, the 4th parameter is always zero.
